About Dr. Miller

Dr. Sarah Miller is a dedicated veterinarian with over 12 years of clinical experience. Her journey began with a profound love for animals, leading her to graduate at the top of her class from Cornell University's College of Veterinary Medicine. She specializes in small animal surgery and complex internal medicine cases.

She believes that every pet deserves compassionate, high-level care that balances medical excellence with a gentle touch. At Paws & Whiskers, she leads the surgical department, focusing on minimally invasive techniques to ensure faster recovery for your beloved companions.
